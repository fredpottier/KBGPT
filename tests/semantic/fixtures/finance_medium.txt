Q3 2023 Financial Report

Revenue Analysis

Total revenue for Q3 2023 reached $12.5M, representing a 15% increase year-over-year. This growth was driven primarily by increased sales in the enterprise segment.

Key Performance Indicators:
- Gross Margin: 68%
- Operating Margin: 22%
- Net Profit Margin: 18%

The company's EBITDA improved to $3.2M, up from $2.8M in Q3 2022. This improvement reflects better cost management and operational efficiency gains.

Cash Flow:
Operating cash flow was positive at $2.1M, providing sufficient liquidity for planned investments in R&D and market expansion.

Recommendations:
Management should continue focusing on high-margin enterprise clients while maintaining cost discipline.
